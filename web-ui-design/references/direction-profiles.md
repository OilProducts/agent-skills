# Direction Profiles

Use one profile as the base direction, then tune tokens and patterns without changing the core intent.

## Precision & Density

- Feel: technical, efficient, focused.
- Best for: dashboards, admin tools, developer products.
- Spacing bias: compact.
- Depth bias: borders-only or very subtle shadows.
- Typography bias: smaller sizes, strong tabular alignment.

## Warmth & Approachability

- Feel: friendly, calm, collaborative.
- Best for: consumer tools, education, team workflows.
- Spacing bias: generous.
- Depth bias: subtle shadows and soft radii.
- Typography bias: readable, comfortable body text.

## Sophistication & Trust

- Feel: premium, restrained, dependable.
- Best for: fintech, enterprise settings, risk-sensitive interfaces.
- Spacing bias: moderate and deliberate.
- Depth bias: low-contrast layering with disciplined accents.
- Typography bias: clear hierarchy, moderate contrast.

## Boldness & Clarity

- Feel: decisive, energetic, high signal.
- Best for: ops centers, campaign tools, action-heavy UIs.
- Spacing bias: medium with clear grouping.
- Depth bias: stronger contrast with strict control.
- Typography bias: assertive headings, concise labels.

## Utility & Function

- Feel: practical, minimal, low ornament.
- Best for: internal tools, forms, operational CRUD interfaces.
- Spacing bias: utilitarian and grid-first.
- Depth bias: mostly borders and tonal shifts.
- Typography bias: neutral, compact, legible.

## Data & Analysis

- Feel: analytical, structured, scan-friendly.
- Best for: BI surfaces, reporting, monitoring.
- Spacing bias: tight for tables, looser around summaries.
- Depth bias: subtle sections, low visual noise.
- Typography bias: strong numeric legibility, tabular support.
